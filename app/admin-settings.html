Data Control Center

Manage global limits, backup operations, and access the Danger Zone.

General Configuration

These configurations apply globally across the LMS platform.

Danger Zone

Actions performed here are permanent and cannot be undone without a full database restoration from an offline snapshot.

Clear All Test Results

Permanently deletes every test submission and grade calculated across all courses.

Purge Attendance Records

Wipes the entire attendance database. Usually performed at the beginning of a new Academic Year.

Obliterate All Courses

Instantly deletes every course, strictly severing all enrollments, tests, and attendance attached to them.

Purge All Students

Permanently deletes every student account from both Firebase Authentication and the database — enrollments, attendance, test results, and submissions across all courses. Teacher and Admin accounts are preserved.

Factory Reset System

Wipes all data except the Super Admin account and currently registered Teachers. Erases all students and courses.
